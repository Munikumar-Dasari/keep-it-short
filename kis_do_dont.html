Do's

Diverse Talents: Assemble a team with a mix of skills – from directing and editing to cinematography and storytelling.

Team Dynamics: Nominate members who communicate effectively, work well under pressure, and bring positive energy to the table.

Commitment to Excellence: Choose team members dedicated to delivering top-notch work, adhering to deadlines, and embracing the spirit of the contest.

Inclusive Collaboration: Encourage diversity and inclusivity within your team, fostering a creative environment where every voice is valued.

Cinematic Passion: Nominate individuals who share a genuine love for filmmaking and are excited to contribute their best to the project.

Don'ts

Lone Rangers: Avoid nominating individuals who prefer working in isolation. The contest thrives on teamwork!

Communication Gaps: Steer clear of team members who struggle with effective communication or fail to address conflicts constructively.

Procrastination Pals: Nominate those committed to timely and efficient work, minimizing the risk of last-minute chaos.

Creative Dominance: Say no to team members who aren’t open to collaborative input or disregard the ideas of others.

Negative Energy: Keep negativity at bay by nominating individuals who contribute to a positive and uplifting team atmosphere.
